About Us

About Us

In 1992, The Pretty Peony opened its doors for the first time in bright and sunny Naples, Florida by the Leopold sisters. Their passion for floristry was sparked their mother, Susan, as they could never recall a moment where she was not tending to the flowers in her garden. Their backyard constantly bloomed with beautiful black-eyed Susans, crimson roses, and fresh peonies; filling their house with delicious fragrance and their hearts with warmth.

Since opening their first store front, the Leopold sisters have taken their passion nationwide; expanding into major cities such as Los Angeles, Chicago, and Philadelphia. While their focus remains heavily on wedding & event arrangements, they have also expanded their menu to offer a personalized weekly arrangement service; curated by our best designers to fit your home needs.

Our mission is to bring you beautiful flower arrangements to fit every budget and occassion. All of our flowers are locally hand-picked by each of our designers to ensure we are delivering the most vibrant, fresh flowers. We love to curate our designs specifically to your needs and taste - which is why all of our arrangements are made to order.
